CPANEL & WHM DOCUMENTATION

The Cpanel interface is a web based control panel, which allows our customers to easily control their accounts. With the touch of a button, you can add e-mail accounts, access your files, backup, setup a shopping cart, and much more. The WebHost Manager is an interface we provide to our resellers to control their clients' accounts on our servers. Through WebHost Manager you can add/remove accounts on a server, park or point domains, control bandwidth, disk space, and more.

With both Cpanel and WebHost Manager, you and your clients will be in control of every aspect of the Web Hosting World. With an administrator interface through WebHost Manager, and an end user interface through Cpanel, every part of your hosting operations are covered.
